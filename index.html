Profile

Femi Falana is the MD/CEO of FALAS FYB GLOBAL SERVICES LIMITED, producer of Dara Sachet Pepper & Dara Sachet Crayfish. Holds an HND in Mechanical Engineering from the Federal Polytechnic Ado Ekiti, Ekiti State and currently studying to become a reputable Frontend Developer with slack ID~ femifalanatv. Born on 26th March, 1986, Femi Falana hails from Akure, Ondo State.

He has cut his niche in the Consumer Goods and Real Estate sectors of the economy. He has over 10 years working experience, mainly in Production and Project/Facilities Management having worked with FTN Cocoa Processors PLC and Afriland Properties PLC. Femi Falana is also an Associate Member of Institute of Strategic Management, Nigeria. Prior to setting up Falas FYB Global Services Limited, Femi Falana worked with Multiverse PLC, a mining company where he was the Field Engineer. His hobbies include watching football and surfing the internet. Femi Falana has interest in web development and sports.
